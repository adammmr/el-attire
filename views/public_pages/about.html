Our Mission

Milestones

Our Journey

2010

The Beginning

Founded in a small workshop in Lagos with just two tailors and a vision to redefine Nigerian menswear. The first years were spent perfecting our craft and building relationships with local fabric artisans across the country.

2015

First Studio Expansion

Opened our flagship studio on Victoria Island, Lagos. Introduced precision measurement technology alongside traditional techniques. Grew our team to 12 master tailors and expanded our fabric sourcing network internationally.

2020

International Recognition

Showcased our collection at Milan Fashion Week, bringing Nigerian tailoring to the global stage. Expanded fabric sourcing to Italy, Turkey, and India while maintaining our deep Nigerian roots.

2024

Digital Transformation

Launched our comprehensive online platform, bringing bespoke tailoring to clients worldwide. Introduced virtual consultations, interactive measurement tools, and global shipping.

2026

The Future

Continuing to innovate with sustainable fabric sourcing, expanded ready-to-wear collections, and plans for a Milano studio. Our commitment to quality and tradition remains unwavering.

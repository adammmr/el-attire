Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tailoring services, fabrics, orders, and more.

How long does bespoke tailoring take?

Bespoke tailoring typically takes 10-21 business days depending on the complexity of the garment and current workload. Premium and Concierge tier orders receive priority processing. You'll receive a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Can I make changes after placing a tailoring order?

Minor adjustments can be made within 48 hours of placing your order. Significant design changes after production has begun may incur additional costs and extend the delivery timeline. Contact your tailor immediately if you need to make changes.

How do I provide my measurements?

You can provide measurements in three ways: 1) Visit our Lagos or Abuja studio for professional in-person measurement, 2) Schedule a virtual consultation where we guide you through the process, or 3) Enter your measurements manually in your account profile using our interactive measurement guide.

What if my measurements are incorrect?

We strongly recommend professional measurement for the best fit. If you provide self-measurements that result in an ill-fitting garment, we offer one complimentary adjustment. Additional alterations may be charged. Our master tailors review all measurements before production begins.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and mobile money payments. For bespoke orders over $500, a 50% deposit is required to begin work, with the balance due upon completion.

Can I cancel my order?

Ready-to-wear orders can be cancelled within 24 hours for a full refund. Bespoke tailoring orders: deposits are non-refundable once production has begun. Contact our support team immediately if you need to cancel.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 30 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees imposed by their country.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order status in real-time from your account dashboard under "My Orders." For tailoring orders, you can monitor progress through each stage of production.

Can I request fabric samples?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary fabric swatches for most of our collection. You can request up to 5 swatches per order through our fabric page. Swatches typically arrive within 5-7 business days. This helps you feel the texture and see the true color before committing.

Where do your fabrics come from?

Our fabrics are sourced from premium suppliers in Nigeria (Aso Oke, Adire), Italy (damask, silk), Turkey (cotton blends), and India (brocade, embroidery). We personally vet all suppliers to ensure quality, ethical production, and authentic materials.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple and free. Click "Sign In" on the top menu, then select "Create Account." You'll need your phone number (which serves as your username), email address, and a password. Once registered, you can save measurements, track orders, and manage your profile.

What's the difference between your service tiers?

Essential ($95): Standard measurement, basic fabric selection, 1 fitting, 2-week turnaround.
Premium ($195): Precision measurement, premium fabric consultation, 2 fittings, 10-day turnaround, embroidery options.
Concierge ($495): Home visit measurement, exclusive fabric sourcing, unlimited fittings, 5-day priority turnaround, custom embroidery & beading, personal stylist.
